NEDBANK

Nedbank Group is a financial services group in South Africa offering wholesale and retail banking services as well as insurance, asset management, and wealth management. Nedbank Limited is a wholly owned subsidiary of Nedbank Group. Nedbank's primary market is South Africa. Nedbank also operates in six other countries in the Southern African Development Community (SADC), through subsidiaries and banks in Eswatini.

What are Nedbank values?

Integrity - Being honest, trustworthy, consistent and transparent in all our actions and decisions. Respect - Recognising the inherent worth of every individual and treating everyone with dignity. Accountability - Being prepared to take ownership of and be held accountable for our commitments and actions.
